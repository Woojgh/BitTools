How to Use

Poll Text: This will display your poll question or title.

Text Color: This will change the color of all the text in your poll.

Choice Background Color: This choses what color will be the background or your choice bar.

Google Font: You can choose any Google font that you like as long as you copy the name from Google fonts.

How Many Poll Choices: This is for how many poll choices you would like.

Poll Color: This changes what your poll choice fill color will be.

Save All: This will save the state of your current poll.

Reset Form: This will clear the form inputs.

Browser Source Url: This is your dynamic Url that will apply as your browser source.

Launch: This will launch a test of your poll in a separate green screen window.

Logout: This will log you out of our app and your witch authorization.
